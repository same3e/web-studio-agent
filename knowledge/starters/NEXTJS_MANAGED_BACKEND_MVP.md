# NEXTJS_MANAGED_BACKEND_MVP

## Decision profile

Use for a speed-focused authenticated MVP with conventional CRUD and managed auth/database. Baseline: Next.js, managed Postgres/auth/storage, hosted deployment; validate type/lint/build, auth, authz, persistence, first journey. Do not use for unusual compute/realtime/offline constraints without review. Limitation: provider coupling and policy design matter.
