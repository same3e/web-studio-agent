# Content ledger and placeholders

Public-facing claims must be traceable in `.studio/CONTENT_LEDGER.md` to confirmed user input, trusted project material, an explicitly marked placeholder, or an approved provisional statement that is not presented as a business fact.

Never invent names, contacts, addresses, statistics, prices, ratings, reviews, guarantees, medical outcomes, certifications, integrations, GitHub URLs, stars, user counts, or customer logos. A non-functional form may not show a fake successful submission; a placeholder messenger may not pretend to open a real messenger; a missing policy may not look like a working legal link.
