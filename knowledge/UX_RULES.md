# UX rules

- Make the primary action visible early and repeat it at natural decision points.
- Keep navigation and forms concise; provide clear states and accessible labels.
- Preserve readable line lengths, touch targets, focus states, and reduced-motion behavior.
- Use mobile-specific prioritization, imagery, and CTA placement.
- Do not trade basic usability or performance for visual novelty.
- For hero content order, follow `DESIGN_RULES.md`: begin directly with H1 unless a user-approved exception exists.
